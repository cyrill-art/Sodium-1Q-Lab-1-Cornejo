Himalayan Blue Poppies

The Himalayan blue poppy (Meconopsis) is a striking perennial known for its vibrant, almost luminous, blue flowers with a contrasting golden center. These plants are native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas, thriving in cool, moist, and shaded environments. They are characterized by their large, poppy-like blooms and hairy, toothed leaves. While beautiful, they can be challenging to cultivate outside their natural habitat due to their specific temperature and soil requirements.
